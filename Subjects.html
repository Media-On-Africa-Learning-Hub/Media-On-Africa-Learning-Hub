Assessment Rubric

This rubric outlines how performance in Mathematics, Science, and Technology will be evaluated.

Mathematics

Excellent (80–100%): Accurate solutions, clear logical steps, advanced problem-solving
Good (60–79%): Mostly correct, minor errors, good reasoning
Basic (40–59%): Partial solutions, limited reasoning, frequent mistakes
Needs Improvement (<40%): Incorrect answers, poor understanding of concepts

Science

Excellent (80–100%): Strong grasp of concepts, precise experiments, insightful analysis
Good (60–79%): Good understanding, experiments mostly correct, reasonable analysis
Basic (40–59%): Basic knowledge, limited experiments, weak analysis
Needs Improvement (<40%): Minimal understanding, inaccurate experiments, poor analysis

Technology

Excellent (80–100%): Innovative designs, functional solutions, excellent technical skills
Good (60–79%): Solid designs, mostly functional, good technical skills
Basic (40–59%): Basic designs, partially functional, limited skills
Needs Improvement (<40%): Poor designs, non-functional, weak technical skills

Art

Excellent (80–100%): Highly creative, strong technique, original ideas, excellent use of materials
Good (60–79%): Creative work with solid technique, some originality, effective use of materials
Basic (40–59%): Limited creativity, basic technique, minimal originality
Needs Improvement (<40%): Weak creativity, poor technique, little effort or originality
