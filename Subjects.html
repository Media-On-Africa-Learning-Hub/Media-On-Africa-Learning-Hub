Assessment Rubric

This rubric outlines how performance in Mathematics, Science, Technology, and Visual Arts will be evaluated.

Criteria Excellent (80–100%) Good (60–79%) Basic (40–59%) Needs Improvement (<40%)
Mathematics Accurate solutions, clear logical steps, advanced problem-solving Mostly correct, minor errors, good reasoning Partial solutions, limited reasoning, frequent mistakes Incorrect answers, poor understanding of concepts
Science Strong grasp of concepts, precise experiments, insightful analysis Good understanding, experiments mostly correct, reasonable analysis Basic knowledge, limited experiments, weak analysis Minimal understanding, inaccurate experiments, poor analysis
Technology Innovative designs, functional solutions, excellent technical skills Solid designs, mostly functional, good technical skills Basic designs, partially functional, limited skills Poor designs, non-functional, weak technical skills
Visual Arts Creative, well-composed, excellent use of techniques Good creativity, solid composition, effective techniques Some creativity, basic composition, limited techniques Lacks creativity, poor composition, weak techniques
