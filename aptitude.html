Aptitude Test

About this Aptitude Test

The Aptitude Test is designed to help learners practise core reasoning skills used in academic assessments and entrance exams. It covers four focused areas: Logical Reasoning, Numerical Ability, Verbal Reasoning, and Abstract Reasoning. Each section contains ten multiple-choice questions with instant scoring and explanations to help students learn from mistakes.

How it works

Click Show Questions for any section to reveal the questions. Choose one answer per question and press Check Answers to see your score and the correct answers. Use the Reset button to try again. Each correct answer is worth one point.

Tips for students

  • Read each question carefully and eliminate obviously wrong options first.
  • Manage your time: spend no more than 1–2 minutes per question on practice runs.
  • Review explanations after checking answers to understand common traps.
