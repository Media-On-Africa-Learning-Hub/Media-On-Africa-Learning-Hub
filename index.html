Africa's Biggest E-learning Platform

We are creating an inclusive and accessible e-learning platform specifically designed for high school learners from disadvantaged backgrounds, including those in rural and township areas. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap by providing high-quality, curriculum-aligned resources that are free, easy to access, and engaging.

Subjects We Offer

We focus on STEM subjects.

Mathematics

  • Algebra: Equations, expressions, and functions
  • Geometry: Shapes, measurements, and spatial reasoning
  • Trigonometry: Triangles, angles, and real-world applications
  • Statistics & Probability: Data analysis, averages, graphs, and predictions
  • Intro to Calculus: Rates of change and basic integrals (at higher grades)
  • Mathematical Modeling: Solving real-life problems using math
  • Computational Math: Estimation, coding, and numerical methods
  • Logic & Problem Solving: Patterns, reasoning, and critical thinking

Science

  • Physics: Motion, forces, energy, electricity, waves, and simple machines
  • Chemistry: Atoms, elements, chemical reactions, acids & bases, and matter
  • Biology: Cells, genetics, human body systems, ecosystems, and evolution
  • Earth & Environmental Science: Climate, natural resources, geology, and sustainability
  • Scientific Method: Observations, hypothesis testing, experiments, and data analysis
  • Technology Integration: Using tools, simulations, and digital labs in investigations
  • STEM Applications: Real-life experiments, innovations, and cross-disciplinary projects

Technology

  • Digital Literacy: Understanding computers, internet safety, and digital tools
  • ICT: Software, hardware, and data handling
  • Coding & Programming: Basics of coding languages (Python, HTML, JavaScript)
  • Robotics & Automation: Building and programming robots, sensors, and control systems
  • Computer Systems: Hardware, software, storage, and networks
  • Cybersecurity: Online safety, data privacy, and responsible tech use
  • Design & Innovation: Creating apps, websites, models, and solutions
  • Emerging Technologies: AI, VR/AR, 3D printing, IoT, and smart systems

Arts

  • Visual Arts: Drawing, painting, sculpture, printmaking, photography, and design
  • Art Elements & Principles: Line, shape, color, texture, space, balance, contrast, rhythm
  • Art History & Theory: Local and global art movements, artists, and cultural influences
  • Media & Digital Arts: Graphic design, animation, video editing, and digital illustration
  • Crafts & Applied Arts: Textile work, ceramics, beadwork, and mixed media
  • Creative Process: Brainstorming, planning, creating, reflecting, and refining artwork
  • Art Critique & Analysis: Describing, interpreting, and evaluating art pieces
  • Careers in the Arts: Design, fashion, animation, and fine arts industries

What Our Students Say

The following testimonial reflects the personal experience of one of our high school students engaged with our e-learning platform. It highlights the platform's impact on academic performance, accessibility, and learner confidence. This feedback demonstrates the value of digital education in township communities, where traditional resources may be limited but learner potential remains high.

Using the Media On Africa Learning Hub e-learning platform has made studying much easier for me. I like being able to learn at my own pace and review lessons when I need to. The videos and quizzes help me understand difficult subjects better, and my grades have improved. I feel more confident and in control of my learning now.

Jasmine T., Grade 11

Before using the e-learning platform, it was hard to keep up, especially when I missed school. Now, I can study anytime, even at home with my phone. The videos explain things clearly, and I feel more prepared for exams. It’s really helped me stay focused and dream bigger about my future.

Lebo M., Grade 10, Soweto
